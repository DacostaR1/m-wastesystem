About the System

The Digital Waste Collection Management and Monitoring System is designed to improve waste collection services through a simple and efficient digital platform.

Residents can submit waste collection requests online, track request progress, and receive timely service updates.

Administrators can approve requests, assign collection trucks, monitor operations, and manage service delivery to ensure cleaner communities.

Services Offered

  • Online waste collection requests
  • Request approval and management
  • Truck assignment and scheduling
  • Monitoring and reporting
  • User and admin access
